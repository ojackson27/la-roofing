# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Homeowners and property/facilities managers in Exeter, Devon, and the surrounding area looking for roofing work: repairs, re-roofs, slate/tiling, industrial cladding, or single-ply flat roofing. They land here either searching locally for a roofer or following up after seeing the van/signage/Google listing, and want fast reassurance that this is a real, reputable, local company before calling or filling in the contact form. Decision drivers: trust signals (reviews, insurance, local presence), visible proof of past work, and an easy way to get in touch.

## Product Purpose

A marketing site for LA Roofing (Exeter) Ltd that converts local search/word-of-mouth visitors into phone calls or contact-form submissions. Success looks like a visitor quickly understanding what LA Roofing does, trusting the company, seeing evidence of real completed work, and reaching out.

## Brand Personality

Trusted, local, sturdy. A family-trade business with deep Exeter/Devon roots and a 4.97 Google rating — the design should feel dependable and built-to-last rather than slick/corporate or trend-chasing. Confidence comes from real photos of real jobs and real reviews, not abstraction.

## Anti-references

No specific named anti-references given. Generally avoid generic SaaS-marketing tropes (hero-metric stat blocks, tiny uppercase eyebrows on every section, identical icon-card grids) that would make a local trade business look like a tech startup.

## Design Principles

- Proof over claims: real job photos and real Google reviews carry more weight than adjectives.
- Local and specific: Exeter/Devon/Pinhoe identity should be visible, not generic "roofing company" copy.
- One clear next action: every page should make calling or contacting obvious and low-friction.
- Sturdy, not flashy: motion and visual flourish should feel solid and intentional, never gimmicky or trend-driven.
- Trust is cumulative: reviews, insurance, and local proof should reinforce each other across the page, not live in one isolated section.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA conformance required: body text ≥4.5:1 contrast, large/bold text ≥3:1. All animations must respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. Touch targets ≥44px (older/non-technical visitors are a meaningful share of users). No reliance on color alone to convey meaning.
